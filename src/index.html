Mass Effect Legendary Edition (release 24 May 2021) is the remaster of Mass Effect trilogy. The trilogy was release on Xbox One, Playstation 4, and PC.Mass Effect Legendary Edition bundles Mass Effect, Mass Effect 2, and Mass Effect 3 together in one launcher. Over 40 single-player DLCs from all three games are included. However, Mass Effect 3 Multiplayer and its corresponding DLCs are not included and neither is Pinnacle Station.
